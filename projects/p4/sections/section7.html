Persian Yogurt

    1/4 black or gold raisins
    1 1/2 cups plain Greek yogurt
    1 cucumber, peeled and finely diced
    1/4 cup any combination finely chopped mint leaves, dill, parsley and cilantro
    1 garlic clove, finely grated or pounded with a pinch of salt
    1/4 cup toasted walnuts, coarsly chopped
    2 tbls extra-virgin olive oil
    A generous pinch of salt

In a small bowl submerge the raisins in boiling water. Let them sit 15 minutes to rehydrate and plump back up. Drain and place in a medium bowl. Add the yogurt, cucumber, herbs, garlic, walnuts, olive oil and salt. Stir to combine, taste, adjust salt as needed.

Chill until serving. Cover and refrigerate up to 3 days.
